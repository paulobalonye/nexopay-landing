FAQ

Pricing questions.

Is there a free trial for Builder?
Yes — every new account starts with $50 in free transaction credits, no credit card required. The Sandbox plan is always free.
What counts as one "transaction"?
Any agent-to-agent payment, escrow release, or withdrawal. Wallet creation, balance checks, and rule updates are all free API calls.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. No lock-in periods on Builder. Enterprise contracts are annual.
What currency do I pay Agntly in?
Monthly subscription fees are billed in USD to a credit card or invoice. Transaction fees are deducted from your org wallet balance in USDC.
